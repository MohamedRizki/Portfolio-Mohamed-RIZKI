About Me

Hi there, my name is Mohamed RIZKI.

I am a graduate of the École Nationale des Sciences Géographiques (ENSG) in Geomatics, specializing in Geo Data Science. My academic background has allowed me to combine advanced technical skills in spatial data management, GIS, remote sensing, and geographic modeling, providing me with a solid foundation to address complex problems across various fields.

Throughout my career, I have had the opportunity to work in diverse sectors such as IT, environmental science, climate change, topography, and telecommunications. These experiences have allowed me to contribute to large-scale projects by integrating geospatial solutions to tackle challenges related to natural resource management, infrastructure optimization, and addressing the effects of climate change.

With my education in geomatics and my diverse professional experiences, I am able to navigate interdisciplinary projects, combining technical expertise and strategic vision to develop innovative solutions. Passionate about information technology and the impact of geospatial analysis, I am committed to making meaningful contributions through innovation and teamwork.

Master's Thesis Project
